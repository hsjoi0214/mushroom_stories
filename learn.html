Cordeceps

About Me:
  • Name: Cordyceps sinensis, Chinese caterpillar fungus, winter worm
  • Scientific Name: Ophiocordyceps sinensis
  • Place of Origin: Tibetan Plateau and Himalayan Mt. Ranges

History:

The earliest documented reference to Ophiocordyceps sinensis dates back to the 7th century AD in the Tang Dynasty of China.

Due to its rarity and perceived potency, Ophiocordyceps sinensis became highly prized in Tibetan medicine and folk traditions

Heath Benefits:

Contains nutrients like Protein, Fiber, Carbohydrates, Vitamin B1,B2,B12,K and Minerals like Copper, Iron, Potassium and Selenium

Said to boost Immunity, improve althletic performance and has anti-imflammtory properties
